## How to add an entry

When you publish a piece of content externally:
1. Add a row to the table above
2. Include: date, platform, title, author handle, URL, status (published/archived)
3. Commit with message: `content: log [platform] post "Title"`

## Notes

- Blog posts in `blog-posts/` are markdown drafts; log them here once published to an external platform
- Slide decks and recordings in `talks-podcasts/` should also be logged here
