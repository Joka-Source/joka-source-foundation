> Full alumni ERP — the parent platform for IIM Calcutta alumni management.

## Overview

AlumERP is the comprehensive alumni management platform for IIM Calcutta. It is the parent product that other Joka projects (including Joka Connect) may integrate with over time. It covers alumni records, events, fundraising, career services, and institutional memory.
